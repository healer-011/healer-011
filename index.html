I'm a designer and developer based in Amman, Jordan, focused on building structured digital systems with strong user experience thinking. I enjoy turning real-world processes into clean, usable interfaces, balancing logic, architecture, and design to create tools that feel simple but work reliably underneath.

My recent work includes a martial arts gym platform designed to streamline booking and scheduling flows, and a skincare app prototype built in Figma that explores a more immersive, “magical” user experience through guided interactions and soft visual design.
